Overview

Caloocan City has a robust education system with a mix of public and private institutions, primary to tertiary level, committed teachers, and community outreach programs. The city government supports scholarships, libraries, and technology integration to uplift learning opportunities for all.

University of Caloocan City

A public university offering undergraduate and graduate programs in technology, business, education, and engineering.

La Consolacion College Caloocan

A private Catholic school known for its quality basic education and humanities programs.

Caloocan High School

Many public elementary schools in Caloocan implement reading and numeracy programs supported by NGOs and local government.
